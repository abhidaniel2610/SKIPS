About The Institute

St.Kabir Institute of Professional Studies {SKIPS} was set up in 2008 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat under the guidance of St.Kabir Group of Education Institute which has been operating since 1985.

St.Kabir Group of Institute have been scattering the light of information and providing an excellent education for more than 34 years. Presently, it has 5 academic institutes with

6500 students and around 500 academicians, and about 9000 strong alumni.

The institute offers a 2-year full-time PGDM which is AICTE approved with dual specializations for ambitious enterpreneurs and leaders. The institute is situated in one of the most accessible

locations of Ahmedabad. The curriculum includes both theoretical and practical knowledge. The campus offers various world-class facilities to its students.
